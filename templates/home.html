Welcome to the Dice Game!

The Dice Game is a two-player game where you accumulate points by rolling a dice.
The first player to reach the winning point threshold (default 20) wins.

Enter the names of the two players to start the game. On your turn, click the "Roll Dice" button to roll the dice. Your score will increase by the value of the dice roll, but be careful - if you roll a 1, your score will be reset to 0, and your turn will end.

You can choose to "Hold" your score at any point during your turn, which will pass the turn to the other player. The game will continue until one player reaches the winning point threshold. Good luck and have fun!üòä
